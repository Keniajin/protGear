WELCOME TO protGear

An interactive open source software tool for protein micro-array data management in R.

A flexible generic guide and data-processing suite for protein microarrays in R with statistical functions for the above procedures based on our custom protein microarray KILchip v1.0. Preprint available here: http://keniajin.com

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH protGear:

  • Background correction
  • Coefficient of Variation. 
  • Normalisation. 
  • Compare normalisation techniques.

Look out for  for additional techniques. 

This shiny app loads protein microarray data and sample id files.
